Creating a fruit bowl in Blender

The Planning

For my bowl I wanted to select a theme or a general concept and build the components around that idea. I chose to represent the concept of Sour and Harsh. I tried out different layouts and styles, but in the end I decided I wanted it to have a muddy and messy look as if it was made from clay dripping.

Building the Bowl

To create my bowl I used an Open-Source Software called Blender. First, I created a circle plane and extruded its vertices to create the base structure of my 3D model. After this, I started experimenting and modelling the bowl in the Sculpt Mode. Once I was satisfied with my bowl I created and sculpted the fruit with the same process, starting from 3D objects like spheres and cylinders.

At this point, I placed three different light sources around the fruit bowl, to recreate a "sour" mood. All three lights have a different color to enhance the contrasts and shadows on the objects.


Cycles rendering


In the end, I created an animation that showed the bowl rotating on a plane and exported it as Mp4.
